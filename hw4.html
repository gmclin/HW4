A Little About Me

Hello, my name is Gina McLin and this is the story about me. You might find it rather dull as opposed to interesting. Whatever the case, I hope that you will take the time to explore. Thank you.

1. Background

I am one of six siblings, five girls, and one boy. I am usually the quiet one in the family and apparently the all-knowing wizard as well. Although the baby girl, I find myself being the go-to person in the family, whether it is for homework, taxes, personal advice, or even things I have no idea about. When I have time to myself, I enjoy curling up on the couch with a large mug of coffee reading a book, or surfing the web. Three of my major passions I acquired at a very early age. One is the love of playing piano (even though I don’t know how to read notes); another is the space sciences, and reading. I enjoy the peace and beauty of morning sunrises and listening to the birds sing.
